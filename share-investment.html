About Share Investment in South Africa ▼

  • Shares represent ownership in a business, giving you voting rights & potential profit sharing.
  • Shareholders are protected by the Companies Act (No. 71 of 2008).
  • Owning shares means you can attend annual general meetings and vote on company matters.
  • South African law ensures fair treatment and transparency for shareholders.
  • Dividends may be paid out from company profits, at directors’ discretion.
